Student Universe

StudentUniverse is a special blog mainly created for student travelers. Established in 2000, this blog covers travel destinations in the countries of Europe, Asia, South America, Central America, the Middle East, Africa, South Pacific, and New Zealand.

This blog is US-based but has also established separate sites for their UK and Australia-based audience.

StudentUniverse is extremely helpful to those students who may need to travel. These students either travel for adventure, the holidays, or during summer break.
